ABOUT US

HARVARD IS FAMOUS FOR ITS TOP FACULTY AND ITS RICH HISTORY OF PRODUCING GRADUATES
THAT CHANGED THE COURSE OF HISTORY LIKE
Barack Obama, Natalie Portman, Mark Zuckerberg, Bill Gates, John F. Kennedy, Ruth Bader Ginsburg, T. S. Eliot, and Neil deGrasse Tyson.

Havard's Vision

Harvard University's mission is to provide a transformative liberal arts and sciences education that prepares students to become thoughtful leaders who contribute meaningfully to society
It encourages free thinking, discovery, and critical reflection to help students reach their full potential and develop lifelong learning habits

Student's AIM

Harvard students aim to grow intellectually, think critically, explore diverse ideas, and develop independence
They seek to realize their full potential and prepare to lead and serve society responsibly.

Teacher's AIM

Harvard teachers aim to inspire intellectual curiosity, foster critical thinking, and advance knowledge through teaching and research
They are committed to supporting students' success and preparing them to become ethical leaders who can positively impact society
